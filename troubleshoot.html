Troubleshooting

I can't log into my account. What should I do?

Check if you are entering the correct email address and password. If you forgot your password, use the "Forgot Password" link to reset it.

How do I reset my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

How do I create a new account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, fill in the required information, and follow the instructions to complete the registration process.

I'm not receiving the verification email. What should I do?

Check your spam/junk folder first. If it's not there, try resending the verification email or go back to our chat support and ask for live chat for further assistance.

What are the requirements for creating a password?

Your password should be at least 6 characters long, contain atleast 1 both upper and lower case letters, a number, and a special character.

How do I update my profile information?

Log in to your account, go to the "Profile" section, and edit your information as needed.
