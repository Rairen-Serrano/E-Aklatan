Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 13, 2023

Thank you for using E-Aklatan, the electronic library system of Makati City Public Library. E-Aklatan is committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we and protect it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

E-Aklatan may collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Contact Number
  • Library Cards Details
  • Users Account Information

1.2 Usage Data

We may also collect non-personal information, including but not limited to:

  • System Usage Data
  • Device Information
  • Browser Type
  • IP Address

2. How We Use Your Information

E-Aklatan collects and uses your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Managing and Improving the E-Aklatan System
  • Providing Library Services
  • Notifying Users of Updates and Changes
  • Complying with Legal Requirments

3. Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173)

E-Aklatan adheres to the Data Privacy Act of 2012 in the Philippines, ensuring the protection of your personal information. This includes:

  • Implementing Organizational, Physical, and Technical Security Measures
  • Obtaining Consent for Data Processing
  • Informing Users of the Purpose of Data Collection
  • Allowing Users to Access, Correct, and Delete their Personal Information

4. Security Measures

E-Aklatan employs industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

E-Aklatan may use third-party services to enhance functionality. These services may have their own privacy policies, and users are encouraged to review them.

6. Children's Privacy

E-Aklatan is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

E-Aklatan reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or our services. Users will be notified of significant changes.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us at makaticitylibrary@gmail.com.

By using E-Aklatan, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
