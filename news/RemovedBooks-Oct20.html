Database Update: Removed Books
October 20, 2023       By: Cardo Dalisay

On October 20, 2023, a meticulous review of our library collection led to the removal of several books to ensure the continued relevance and quality of our offerings. The following titles, along with their respective authors and publication dates, were among those bid farewell:

Excised Books:

  • "Outdated Histories: A Retrospective Analysis" by Jane Doe (1998)
  • "Antique Technologies: A Comprehensive Guide" by John Smith (2005)
  • "Obsolete Philosophies in the Modern Era" by Amanda Johnson (2010)
  • "Fictional Fads: Trends That Didn't Stand the Test of Time" by Robert Jones (2013)
  • "Dated Dystopias: Predictions Gone Awry" by Emily White (2016)
While each of these books has played a role in our collection, this thoughtful selection process reflects our commitment to maintaining a dynamic library that meets the evolving needs and interests of our valued readers.
