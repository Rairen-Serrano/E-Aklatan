Newly Published Newspaper
September 1, 2023       By: Cardo Dalisay

Makati City is proud to introduce its newly published city newspaper, a dynamic and informative platform designed to keep residents and visitors alike abreast of the latest developments, events, and community highlights.

This publication serves as a vital communication channel, fostering a sense of unity and shared identity within the bustling urban landscape of Makati. The newspaper covers a wide array of topics, from local government initiatives and civic engagement opportunities to cultural happenings and profiles of notable residents, making it an invaluable resource for staying connected with the vibrant heartbeat of this dynamic city.
