National Friends of Library Day 2023
October 15-21, 2023       By: Cardo Dalisay

During the week of October 15 to 21, 2023, communities nationwide united in celebration of National Friends of Libraries Day, a dedicated period to acknowledge and honor the remarkable contributions of library supporters. This special week underscored the pivotal role played by Friends of Libraries groups in sustaining and enriching local libraries. From organizing book sales, fundraising events, and community outreach programs to offering essential volunteer support, these dedicated individuals and organizations demonstrated unwavering commitment to the preservation and growth of their cherished libraries.

Throughout the celebratory week, libraries and their Friends groups engaged in a myriad of activities, such as author talks, book signings, and special programs, aimed at bringing communities together to celebrate the vital role libraries play in our lives.

The events served not only as a recognition of the profound impact of these advocates but also as an opportunity for communities to come together and reaffirm their commitment to the enduring importance of libraries in shaping a well-informed and connected society. National Friends of Libraries Day stands as a testament to the collective power of individuals and groups dedicated to nurturing and preserving the literary and educational treasures that libraries offer to communities across the nation.
