Makati City Government

Vision

Makati shall lead the Philippines into the 21st century: its global and national enterprise, leading the creation of a new, responsible and sustainable economy; its citizen, productive, empowered and God-loving.

Mission

To acquire a strong impact on the public, the Makati City Library provides total quality service, through fast and efficient access to information that builds positive insights, attitudes and values, helping clientele develop into informed, free and peace-loving citizens.
